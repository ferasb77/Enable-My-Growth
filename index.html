What if the accepted explanation is incomplete?

I am Feras Banna — a certified Hogan assessor, leadership consultant, and facilitator with more than 25 years of experience working across government, energy, banking, healthcare, and private-sector organizations throughout the Middle East and beyond.

Most of that work has involved a recurring observation: organizations explain performance through personality, leaders explain hesitation through motivation, teams explain disagreement through resistance. In almost every case, the explanation arrives before the conditions have been adequately examined.

The ideas on this site emerged from that experience. They are not abstract philosophy. They are conclusions drawn from years of watching what actually shapes behavior — and what gets overlooked when we move too quickly to explain it.

My work is built around challenging that tendency.
