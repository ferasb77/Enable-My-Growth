About

Helping leaders see what others miss.

I am a certified Hogan assessor, leadership consultant, and facilitator with more than two decades of experience helping organizations develop leaders, strengthen decision quality, and navigate complex people challenges. My work spans executive assessment, leadership development, talent strategy, and organizational effectiveness across the Middle East and beyond.

Rather than focusing on motivation or personality labels, I help leaders examine the conditions shaping their decisions, performance, and influence. Through a combination of evidence-based assessment, structured dialogue, and practical challenge, clients gain clearer insight into themselves, their teams, and the environments in which they operate.

This is not therapy. It's not cheerleading. It's a thinking partnership for people who take their growth seriously.
