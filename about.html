Perspective
Most explanations arrive too quickly.

Organizations explain performance through personality. Leaders explain hesitation through motivation. Teams explain disagreement through resistance.

In many cases, the explanation is accepted before the conditions have been examined. My work is built around challenging that tendency — and creating the space for better questions before commitment to an explanation.

What I Do

I help leaders and organizations:

  • Improve decision quality before commitment is required
  • Understand leadership strengths and risks through evidence-based assessment
  • Navigate complexity and uncertainty with greater clarity
  • Build stronger leadership systems and talent practices
  • Examine assumptions before acting on them
