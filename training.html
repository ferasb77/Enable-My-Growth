How the Learning Is Designed

Learning should change more than what people know.

The most valuable learning experiences help people reconsider what they see, strengthen how they think, and apply that shift to the situations they are responsible for.

01

Context before content

The design begins with the organization, audience, and situations participants actually face.

02

Application over information

Concepts are connected to real decisions, relationships, responsibilities, and performance expectations.

03

Constructive challenge

Participants are invited to examine assumptions and consider alternatives—not simply absorb prescribed answers.

04

Transfer into practice

Activities, reflection, and follow-through help the learning survive beyond the workshop.

Hogan Practitioner Development

Specialist workshops for responsible, confident Hogan practice.

In addition to integrating Hogan insights into leadership development, I deliver three dedicated practitioner workshops covering certification, advanced interpretation, and advanced feedback.

Practitioner Foundation

Hogan Certification Workshop

A structured certification experience that develops the knowledge and practical foundation required to interpret and apply Hogan assessment results responsibly.

Advanced Practice

Hogan Advanced Interpretation Workshop

A deeper examination of how assessment scales interact, how patterns should be interpreted, and how context changes the meaning and relevance of a profile.

Advanced Practice

Hogan Advanced Feedback Workshop

Developing the judgment and conversational skill required to translate assessment evidence into constructive, balanced, and development-focused feedback.

Assessment provides evidence and vocabulary. It should deepen the inquiry—not become a verdict about the person.
